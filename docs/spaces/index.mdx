# Our Spaces

Community House offers a variety of private and shared spaces designed for comfortable living and productive work.

## Private Rooms

Each private room comes fully furnished and includes:
- Quality mattress and bedding
- Desk and chair
- Wardrobe/closet space
- Fresh linens and towels
- Smart lock for keyless entry

Choose from rooms with shared or private bathroom access based on your preferences and budget.
