## Our Story

Community House was founded as part of a broader experiment in alternative living and working models. The idea came from experiences with short-term rentals that often lacked reliable workspaces and genuine community connections. This inspired the creation of a space designed for living, working, and growing together.

We are one of many global experiments in coliving and coworking, inspired by the concepts found in **Network States** ‚Äî new ways of living and organizing communities that emphasize both individual and collective growth. Our goal is to foster a shared sense of purpose while supporting personal development.

Coliving at Community House isn't just about saving on costs. It's about creating a sense of community and trust. By offering a stable, community-oriented living environment, we aim to provide an alternative to transient vacation rentals that supports longer-term relationships and collaborations.

## The Experiment

We view Community House as an ongoing experiment, always adapting and improving based on what we learn from our residents and the broader coliving community. We are connected to other coliving and coworking experiments around the world, sharing ideas and inspiration.

### What We're Building

- **Alternative Housing Models**: Testing new approaches to urban living that prioritize community over isolation
- **Remote Work Infrastructure**: Creating spaces optimized for distributed teams and independent workers  
- **Governance Experiments**: Exploring collaborative decision-making and self-organization
- **Sustainability Initiatives**: Working toward environmentally conscious living practices
