# About The Fellowship

The Fellowship is a coliving community in Travis Heights, Austin. Three houses on a half-acre, shared by people who value community, convenience, and a thoughtfully designed living environment.

---

## The Property

Our half-acre property includes three houses with private bedrooms and shared common spaces. The outdoor areas connect the houses, creating a neighborhood within a neighborhood.

**The Houses**
- Private bedrooms ranging from small to master-sized
- Fully equipped kitchens in each house
- Shared living and dining areas
- High-speed internet throughout

**Shared Amenities**
- Private gym
- Cold plunge and hot tub
- Movie theatre
- House Tesla
- Backyard chickens
- Half-acre of outdoor space

---

## The Location

Travis Heights is one of Austin's most established neighborhoods, known for tree-lined streets, historic homes, and walkability. We're less than a mile from Lady Bird Lake Trail and walking distance to South Congress.

---

## The Community

The Fellowship brings together professionals, creatives, and entrepreneurs who appreciate both community and independence. Residents have their own private space while sharing access to amenities that would be impractical to have alone.
