## What's Included

All memberships include:

- **High-Speed Internet** — 1 Gbps symmetric fiber, WiFi & Ethernet
- **Utilities** — Electricity, water, gas, trash
- **Fully Equipped Kitchen** — All appliances and cookware
- **Cleaning Services** — Regular cleaning of shared spaces
- **Laundry Facilities** — On-site washer and dryer
- **Community Events** — Workshops, dinners, and networking
- **Outdoor Spaces** — Access to deck and backyard
- **Smart Home Features** — Keyless entry, climate control

---

## Terms

- **Minimum Stay**: 1 month
- **Lease Type**: Month-to-month (no long-term commitment required)
- **Move-in**: First month + security deposit (1 month)
- **Notice Period**: 30 days
