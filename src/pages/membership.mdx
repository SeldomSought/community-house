## Utilities

Utilities typically run **$100-150 per person** per month and include electricity, water, gas, and trash.

Partial furnishings are available if helpful.

---

## What's Included

All rooms include full access to community amenities:

- **1-5 Gbps Fiber Internet** — Blazing fast throughout all three houses
- **The Conservatory** — Spacious room for gathering, working, or music
- **Hot Tub & Cold Plunge** — Wellness and recovery on-site
- **Theatre Room** — Projector, screen, and sound for movie nights and karaoke
- **Shared Self-Driving Tesla** — Community vehicle for errands and exploring
- **Barbell Gym** — Full setup, no membership needed
- **Backyard Chickens** — Fresh eggs from our mobile coop
- **Guest Loft** — Dedicated space for hosting visitors
- **Woodwork Shed** — Tools and space for projects
- **Half Acre Outdoor Space** — Fire pits, propane grill, room for parties
- **3 Kitchens** — Fully equipped across all three houses
- **Multiple Living Rooms** — Plenty of hang out spaces

---

## The Community

We are healthy, conscientious people including grad students, artists, and technologists. We host gatherings such as:

- Parties and performances
- Band practice and music sessions
- Tea parties and salons/discussions
- Movie nights and karaoke
- Regular "family dinners"

You're welcome to participate or not as you choose—there's always plenty of quiet space for introverts.

---

## Location

Travis Heights, Austin — serene and quiet while close to everything:

- **3-10 minutes** to downtown, Town Lake, South Congress, East Austin by car
- Easy access by bike or scooter
- Walking distance to trails and local spots
