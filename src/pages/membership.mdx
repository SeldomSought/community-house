## What's Included

All rooms include full access to community amenities:

- **Gym** — On-site fitness equipment, available 24/7
- **Cold Plunge & Hot Tub** — Recovery and relaxation
- **Movie Theatre** — Dedicated screening room
- **House Tesla** — Shared vehicle for errands and exploring Austin
- **Backyard Chickens** — Fresh eggs from our flock
- **High-Speed Internet** — 1 Gbps symmetric fiber
- **Utilities** — Electricity, water, gas, trash
- **Fully Equipped Kitchens** — All appliances and cookware
- **Laundry Facilities** — On-site washer and dryer
- **Half-Acre Outdoor Space** — Gardens and gathering areas

## Location

The Fellowship is located in Travis Heights, one of Austin's most desirable neighborhoods:

- Less than 1 mile to Lady Bird Lake Trail
- Walking distance to South Congress shops and restaurants
- Easy access to downtown Austin

---

## Terms

- **Minimum Stay**: 1 month
- **Lease Type**: Month-to-month
- **Move-in**: First month + security deposit (1 month)
- **Notice Period**: 30 days
