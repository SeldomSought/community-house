# Travis Heights, Austin

The Fellowship is located in Travis Heights, one of Austin's most established and desirable neighborhoods. Our half-acre property sits less than a mile from Lady Bird Lake Trail, with easy access to South Congress, downtown, and everything Austin has to offer.

---

## The Neighborhood

Travis Heights is a tree-lined residential neighborhood south of Lady Bird Lake. Known for its historic bungalows, friendly community, and walkability, it's one of Austin's most sought-after areas.

### What's Nearby

**Lady Bird Lake Trail** — Less than 1 mile
- 10+ miles of hiking and biking trails
- Kayak and paddleboard rentals
- Running and outdoor fitness

**South Congress (SoCo)** — Walking distance
- Local boutiques and vintage shops
- Restaurants and coffee shops
- Live music venues

**Downtown Austin** — 10 minutes
- Tech companies and startups
- Entertainment district
- Convention center
