Features & Amenities

Everything you need for productive work and comfortable living, all under one roof.

Three Houses, One Community

The Fellowship spans three houses on a half-acre property, creating a village-like atmosphere with private spaces and shared amenities.

Private Gym

Stay fit with our on-site gym, available 24/7 for residents. No membership fees, no crowded equipment—just show up and work out.

Cold Plunge & Hot Tub

Recover and relax with our cold plunge and hot tub. Perfect for post-workout recovery or unwinding after a long day.

Home Movie Theatre

Enjoy movie nights in our dedicated theatre room with comfortable seating and a big screen. Host watch parties or catch up on your favorites.

House Tesla

Need to run errands or explore Austin? The community Tesla is available for residents to use, making car-free living easy.

Backyard Chickens

Fresh eggs from our backyard flock. Connect with nature and enjoy farm-fresh breakfast without leaving home.

Half-Acre Outdoor Space

Spread out across a half-acre of outdoor space. Gardens, gathering areas, and room to breathe in the heart of Travis Heights.

Steps from Lady Bird Trail

Less than a mile to the Lady Bird Lake Trail. Morning runs, evening walks, and kayaking all within easy reach.

Flexible Month-to-Month Leases

No long-term commitments required. Month-to-month leases give you the flexibility to stay as long as you need.

Blazing Fast Internet

Business-grade 1 Gbps symmetric fiber throughout all three houses. Work remotely without interruption.

Fully Equipped Kitchens

Each house has a fully stocked kitchen. Cook your own meals or join community dinners with fellow residents.

Safety & Security

Exterior cameras, fenced property, and smart locks on each bedroom. Peace of mind in a welcoming community.
